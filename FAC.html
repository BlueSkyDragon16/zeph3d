About this Project.

The cap is specifically crafted to fit Anycubic and Elegoo resin bottles, offering both elegance and functionality. Its thoughtful design incorporates a built-in funnel, simplifying the process of pouring resin back into the bottle. As I continue refining this design, my goal is to create a unified, one-piece structure for improved ease of 3D printing. This iterative approach ensures a seamless and efficient user experience in future versions. In addition, I plan to integrate a mesh layer into the design. This added feature will enhance the cap by providing filtration, preventing any unwanted particles from entering the resin during the pouring process. This iterative improvement aims to further optimize the functionality and reliability of the cap for Anycubic resin bottles.

Things used in the project include:
  1. 3D Printer
  2. Resin Bottle (AnyCubic or Elegoo)
  3. Filter
