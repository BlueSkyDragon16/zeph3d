About this Project.

Embracing the resourcefulness of being a university student on a budget, I leveraged my existing 3D printer to fashion a stand that perfectly positions the phone for the new iPhone features. The initial success prompted me to consider sharing this solution with others, leading me to enhance the design for compatibility with a range of circular wireless chargers. This modification ensures that the stand can cater to diverse charging options, making it a versatile and accessible solution for a broader audience.

Things used in the project include:
  1. 3D Printer
  2. Cheap Wireless Charger
